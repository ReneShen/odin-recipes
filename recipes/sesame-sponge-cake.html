Sesame Sponge Cake

Bake: 350*F 9 mins. (rotate) /10 mins.

Yield: 3 cake rolls OR 30 cupcakes(1.1 scoop/cupcake)

Recipe

  • Egg white 180g
  • Sugar 84g

  • Egg yolk 201g
  • Sugar2 42g
  • Honey 36g

  • Flour 50g
  • Sesame powder 20g
  • Milk powder 9g

  • Butter 36g
  • Cream 45g

Method

  1. Whip the egg yolk, honey and sugar 2 until ribbon stage.
  2. Whip up egg white and sugar.
  3. Sift all the dry ingredients into the egg yolks.
  4. Heat up the cream and the butter.
  5. Add the slightly hot cream into egg yolk mixture.
  6. Temper some egg whites into egg yolk mixture.
  7. Fold the other two portions of the meringue into the mixture.
  8. Pour onto flat sheet trays with parchment papers OR into cupcake tins with cupcake liners.
  9. Bake the cake.
  10. Decorate as needed.
