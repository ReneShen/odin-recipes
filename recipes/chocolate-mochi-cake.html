Chocolate Mochi Cake

Bake: 350*F 13 mins. (cupcake tins)

Yield: 6 cupcakes(80% full)

Recipe

  • Butter 112g
  • Dark chocolate 100g
  • Salt some

  • Egg 180g
  • Brown sugar 60g

  • Mochiko flour 60g

Method

  1. Melt the chocolate, salt, and butter in a pot(hot but not burn).
  2. Whisk eggs and sugar until well beaten.
  3. Pour in the hot liquid into the egg mixture and whisk well.
  4. Whisk in all the flour.
  5. Pour into cupcake tins with cupcake liners.
  6. Bake the cake.
  7. Decorate as needed.
