Dog shaped hotdog buns

Hotdog Bun Components

  • Bread: Sweet Dough Bread
  • Other Ingredients: 16 Hotdogs, mustard, ketchup, and lettuce(marinated as needed)

Assembling

  1. Cut the room temperature sweet bread in half in side way.
  2. Place some chopped lettuce in bread.
  3. Grill the hotdog and place it into the bread on top of the layer of lettuce.
  4. Add the mustard and ketchup on to the hotdog.
  5. Serve warm.
