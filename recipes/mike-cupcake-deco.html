Monster cupcakes - Mike

Cupcake Components

  • Cake Base: Sesame Sponge Cake
  • Filling: Matcha Chocolate Whipping Cream
  • Other Decorations: Edible eyes decoration and melted dark chocolate

Assembling

  1. Whip up matcha cream until pipable while waiting for the sesame cake to be cold.
  2. Pipe matcha cream filling on top of the sesame cake using a round tip.
  3. Palace the eyes, and pipe the melted dark chocolate as Mike's mouth.
  4. Serve room temperature.
