Sesame Chocolate Whipping Cream

Yield: 21 people

Recipe

  • Whipping cream 1 90g
  • Corn syrup 10g
  • Honey 10g

  • White chocolate 130g
  • Sesame powder 180g
  • Salt some

  • Whipping cream 2 500g

Method

  1. Melt the cream, corn syrup, and honey in a pot(simmer).
  2. Pour the cream into the chocolate, sesame powder, and salt and stir gently until shinny and well blended.
  3. Add in cold cream 2 then put into the fridge and let it sit for at least 30 mins.
  4. Whip the sesame whipping cream right before use.
  5. Use it as fillings or decorations.
