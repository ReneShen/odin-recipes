Blue Chocolate Whipping Cream

Yield: 21 people

Recipe

  • Whipping cream 1 90g
  • Corn syrup 10g
  • Honey 10g

  • White chocolate 130g
  • Salt some

  • Whipping cream 2 500g
  • Blue food color some
  • red food color optional

Method

  1. Melt the cream, corn syrup, and honey in a pot(simmer).
  2. Pour the cream into the chocolate and salt and stir gently until shinny and well blended.
  3. Add in cold cream 2 and food coloring then put into the fridge and let it sit for at least 30 mins.
  4. Whip the chocolate whipping cream right before use.
  5. Use it as fillings or decorations.
