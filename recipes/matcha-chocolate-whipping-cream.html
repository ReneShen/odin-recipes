Matcha Chocolate Whipping Cream

Yield: 21 people

Recipe

  • Whipping cream 1 90g
  • Corn syrup 10g
  • Honey 10g

  • White chocolate 130g
  • Matcha powder 6g
  • Salt some

  • Whipping cream 2 500g
  • Green food coloring optional

Method

  1. Melt the cream, corn syrup, and honey in a pot(simmer).
  2. Pour the cream into the chocolate, matcha powder, and salt and stir gently until shinny and well blended.
  3. Add in cold cream 2 (and food coloring) then put into the fridge and let it sit for at least 30 mins.
  4. Whip the matcha whipping cream right before use.
  5. Use it as fillings or decorations.
