Swiss roll - Totoro

Swiss Roll Components

  • Cake Base: Sesame Sponge Cake
  • Filling: White Chocolate Whipping Cream
  • Decoration: Sesame Chocolate Whipping Cream
  • Other Decorations: Edible eyes decoration, melted dark chocolate, and mint

Assembling

  1. Unpeeled the parchment paper on top of the sesame sponge cake.
  2. Spread the filling(white chocolate whipping cream) on top of the sesame sponge cake.
  3. Gently roll up and tuck the cake into a circle.
  4. Cut the cake rolls into around 2 inches thick.
  5. Pipe the sesame cream decoration as totoro's hands, body marks, and ears.
  6. Paste the eyes, and pipe the melted dark chocolate as totoro's nose and whiskers.
  7. Serve cold.
