Sweet Dough Bread

Bake: 375*F 15-20 mins. (baking tray)

Yield: 16 buns (58g/ each)

Recipe

  • Bread flour 500g
  • Sugar 80g
  • Salt 10g

  • Yeast 6g
  • Milk 120ml
  • Cold water 120ml
  • Egg 2 ea

  • Butter (Room temperature) 60g

  • Egg 3 ea
  • Sugar2 some
  • Salt2 some

Method

    Mix the dough

  1. Place all the dry ingredients into mixer, and mix well using a hook.
  2. Whisk the yeast into all the wet ingredients.
  3. Stream in the wet ingredients to the dry ingredients under slow speed.
  4. Once it becomes a dough, mix the dough under medium speed.
  5. Mix the dough until it's barely sticking to the mixing bowl.
  6. Add in the room temperature butter.
  7. Mix the dough under slow speed until the butter is absorbed completely.
  8. Mix the dough under medium speed until the dough is smooth and forms a thin layer while stretching.

    9. First proofing

  9. Fold the dough into a round shape.
  10. Put the dough into an oiled bowl covered with a wet towel.
  11. Rest the dough for 60 mins.

    12. Divide the dough

  12. Flatten the dough gently to push out the air.
  13. Divide the dough into 16 portion (58g each).

    14. Shape the dough

  14. Round all the dough until the surface is shinny.
  15. Place all the shaped dough on oiled baking tray covered with a wet towel.
  16. Rest all the dough for 15-20 mins.
  17. Flatten the dough into a flat oval shape.
  18. Roll the dough into a rounded-corner-oval shape.
  19. Place all the shaped dough on oiled baking trays (leave space between all of them).
  20. Cut each dough on the side as dog ears.

    21. Final proofing and baking

  21. Cover the trays with wet towels.
  22. Rest the dough until double the size.
  23. Make eyes and nose using black beans.
  24. Brush the dough with egg wash (beaten egg, sugar2, and salt2).
  25. Bake the dough.
  26. Decorate as needed.
