Work Flow

Time: Saturday Morning

  1. Scale chocolate whipping cream recipe X1 (Cream1, honey, and corn syrup).
  2. Separate the rest of the chocolate whipping cream recipe into 4 different containers for 4 different flavors and colors.
  3. Make all 4 creams (Vanilla, Blue, Sesame, Matcha).
  4. Make chocolate mochi cake.
  5. Make sesame sponge cake, and bake in 1 sheet tray, and the rest cupcake tins.
  6. Make sweet dough bread.
  7. While waiting for the dough to proof, whip up the white chocolate cream and spread it on the swiss roll. Put the roll into fridge.
  8. Continue shaping the bread.
  9. While waiting for the dough to final proof, whip up the blue chocolate cream and matcha chocolate cream, and assemble monster cupcakes - Mike and monster cupcakes - James.
  10. Finish baking the bread.
  11. Finish assembling the swiss roll - Totoro.
  12. Finish assembling the dog shaped hotdog buns.
